# Sample Screening Summary

This is the kind of screening artifact `stock-research-desk` writes into:

- `~/Desktop/Stock Research Desk/screenings/*.md`

## Theme

`China robotics`

## Recommendation Summary

The desk first scouts a broad public-web candidate set, then pushes a stricter second-screen committee, then spends full deep-research budget only on the finalists.

Priority names typically look like this:

### SaiTeng `603283.SH`

- Recommendation rank: `A`
- Screen score: `87`
- Research verdict: `watchlist`
- Confidence: `high`
- Why now: automation demand is now overlapping with the semiconductor localization narrative
- Quick take: the name is interesting because upside is tied to a cleaner semiconductor mix shift, not just legacy consumer-electronics automation
- Short-term target: `45.55` | `1-3 months`
- Bull/Bear focus: bull on operating leverage and domestic substitution, bear on customer concentration and narrative overshoot

### Peer Name `XXXXXX`

- Research verdict: `watchlist`
- Confidence: `medium`

- Quick take: worthy of follow-up, but not the first name to spend deep-research time on
